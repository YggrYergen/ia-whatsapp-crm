### P07 — The Value Equation (Four Drivers)

**Source:** Ch. 6 (L1307-1330)  
**Quote:** *"Dream Outcome (↑) × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement (↑) / Time Delay (↓) × Effort & Sacrifice (↓)"*

**Plain Language:** Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice). To increase value: make the dream outcome bigger, make them believe it will work, make it faster, make it effortless. If you can get the bottom to ZERO, value becomes infinite. This is the master framework for everything.

**Tags:** `OFFER` `PRICE` `SALES` `PSYCH`  
**Impact:** 5 — Business-defining  
**Horizon:** H1  
**Priority:** 15

**For You, This Means:**  
Audit every touchpoint of your offer against each driver:
- **Dream Outcome (↑):** "Never miss a lead again. Your AI replies in 3 seconds, 24/7. Converts 40% more inquiries into booked appointments."
- **Perceived Likelihood (↑):** Show the CasaVitaCure data. Real screenshots. Real conversations. "Here's what happened to a business just like yours in the first week."
- **Time Delay (↓):** "Fully operational in 48 hours. You'll see your first AI-handled conversation before Wednesday."
- **Effort & Sacrifice (↓):** "You give me 30 minutes for setup. After that, you just check your dashboard. The AI handles everything else."

**Critical insight:** Your AI CRM demolishes the denominator. Time delay = 48 hours (vs. months to hire/train a receptionist). Effort = near zero (vs. managing another employee). This is where your value equation explodes.

### P26 — Service Guarantee > Refund Guarantee

**Source:** Ch. 15 (L3253-3265)  
**Quote:** *"I have yet to have a single person say a client took them up on [the service guarantee]. If someone does everything you asked them to and doesn't achieve the result, you happily keep working with them."*

**Plain Language:** A service guarantee ("I'll keep working for free until you get X result") is the strongest guarantee that NEVER costs you money. By the time the guarantee period ends, the client either has the result (happy, keeps paying) or is close enough that they don't bother claiming. And if they DO claim, you're just doing more of the work you already do — no cash out.

**Tags:** `SALES` `OFFER`  
**Impact:** 4 — Revenue-multiplying  
**Horizon:** H1  
**Priority:** 12

**For You, This Means:**  
Your guarantee should be: **"Guarantee: If your AI hasn't booked at least 5 real appointments in the first 30 days, we keep working for free until it does."**

Why this works:
- You can almost certainly hit 5 appointments if the business gets ANY WhatsApp traffic
- If you don't hit 5, the problem is likely traffic (not your product), which you can explain
- You never give money back
- It sounds incredibly confident to the prospect
- No one has ever taken Hormozi up on this type of guarantee. It just doesn't happen.

### P30 — Change the Wrapper, Not the Offer

**Source:** Ch. 16 (L3611-3653)  
**Quote:** *"We are not changing the actual offer. We are only changing the wrapping paper."*

**Plain Language:** When offers fatigue, don't rebuild. Most of the time, you only need to change #1-3. The core offer stays the same for years.

**Tags:** `LEAD` `OFFER` `SCALE`  
**Impact:** 3 — Significant advantage  
**Horizon:** H2  
**Priority:** 6

**For You, This Means:**  
Build ONE core offer now. Invest all your energy in getting it right. When it stops converting (months from now), don't panic. Change the Instagram ad creative. Change the headline. Rename the program from "Activación Express" to "Sprint de Ventas IA." Same product, fresh perception. This saves you from the trap of constantly reinventing instead of selling.
