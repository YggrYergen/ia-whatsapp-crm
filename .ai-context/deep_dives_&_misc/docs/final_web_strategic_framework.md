## 2. ATTRACTION OFFERS — 5 Options, Ranked

You said the free audit is "mid." Fair. Here are 5 options ranked by the principles:

### Option 1: "El Comprador Fantasma" (Secret Shopper Test) ⭐ TOP PICK
**What:** You message their WhatsApp Business as a fake customer. You time how long they take to respond (or if they respond at all). Then you send them a screenshot: "Esto es lo que tus clientes experimentan. Respondiste en 47 minutos. Con IA, responderías en 3 segundos."

**Why it's devastating:**
- PS-10 (Fear + Solution): They SEE their own failure. The fear is personal, undeniable, specific.
- PS-02 (Focal = Causal): Their attention is 100% focused on their own slow response
- PS-06 (Reciprocity): You did research on THEIR business, for free, uninvited
- L16 (Personalization): Maximum — it's literally about THEM
- PS-01 (Privileged Moment): The screenshot IS the privileged moment before your ask

**Script:**
```
"Hola [nombre], soy Tomás. Ayer le mandé un mensaje a tu WhatsApp de negocio 
como si fuera un cliente interesado en [su servicio]. Respondieron en 47 minutos.

Tengo una herramienta de IA que hubiera respondido en 3 segundos. Mira: 
[screenshot del chat de pruebas respondiendo instantáneamente].

¿Te interesa ver cómo funcionaría en tu negocio? 15 minutos, sin compromiso."
```

**Cost to you:** 5 minutes per prospect. High-value, low-volume. Use for your TOP 20 prospects.

---

### Option 2: "Pruébalo Tú Mismo" (Live Test Drive) ⭐⭐ BEST VOLUME PLAY
**What:** Your "chat de pruebas" — a working demo they can interact with RIGHT NOW. Send them a link or QR. They text the demo AI. It responds instantly. They feel the magic.

**Why it works:**
- PS-07 (Consistency): The moment they INTERACT with it, they've made a micro-commitment
- PS-13 (Active commitment): They DID something — typed, received, experienced
- MM-04 (Decoy): The free test reveals how good the paid version would be WITH their actual data
- OF-08 (Bottom of value equation): Zero effort, instant result

**Script:**
```
"Hola [nombre], tengo un asistente con IA funcionando que puedes probar ahora mismo.

Mándale un mensaje como si fueras un cliente → [link/QR al chat de pruebas]

Si te gusta lo que ves, te cuento cómo ponerlo a trabajar para tu negocio en 48 horas."
```

**Cost to you:** Near zero. You already have the test chat. This is your VOLUME play — include it in every cold DM. The key learning from the call is they demo during the call together with you guiding, which adds the co-creation element (PS-09).

**The follow-up call structure:**
1. They test the demo chat (micro-commitment ✓)
2. You hop on a 15-min call: "¿Qué te pareció?"
3. You show them how it would work with THEIR specific business data
4. You guide them through the setup flow so they FEEL how easy it is (PS-13: active commitment)
5. Close: "¿Empezamos el lunes o el miércoles?"

## 3. THE SETUP FEE + FREE TRIAL TENSION — Resolved

### The Problem:
Free trial = no setup fee = no runway. But free trial = massive conversion driver.

### The Solution (from the books):

The trial and the setup fee are NOT mutually exclusive. They exist at DIFFERENT stages:

```
ATTRACTION (free)          →    CONVERSION (paid)
──────────────────              ─────────────────
Option 2: Test the demo         Setup fee (150-200K)
(costs you nothing)             + Monthly (79K/4 weeks)
            │                          │
            └─── 15-min call ──────────┘
```

**The trial (Option 2) is NOT "use our product for free for 7 days."** It's "interact with a demo for 5 minutes." There's no free tenant, no free setup, no infrastructure cost. The prospect tests a DEMO, not their own AI. Their own AI only gets built AFTER they pay.

### For Prospects Who Insist on a "Real" Trial:

Use MM-16 (Trial With Penalty) or MM-08 (Pay Less Now):

**Option A — Trial With Penalty (MM-16):**
> "Te lo configuro gratis por 14 días. Solo 3 condiciones: (1) Activas el asistente en 24 horas, (2) Me respondes el reporte diario, (3) Nos reunimos 15 min el día 7. Si cumples las 3 y no te gusta — no pagas nada. Si dejas de cumplir alguna, se aplica un fee de activación de 100K."

This PROTECTS your setup fee because:
- If they fulfill conditions → they've used it for 14 days, they'll buy
- If they DON'T fulfill → they pay 100K (covers your setup cost)
- The conditions ensure they actually USE it (which means they'll see value)

**Option B — Pay Less Now, Pay More Later (MM-08):**
> "Opción 1: Prueba gratis 14 días, después 95K/mes si te quedas. Opción 2: Empieza hoy a 79K/mes — ese precio se queda para siempre, más te incluyo el setup sin costo. ¿Cuál prefieres?"

This preserves cash flow because:
- Option 1 gets their card on file — most won't cancel
- Option 2 gets cash TODAY and frames 79K as a DISCOUNT from 95K
- Either way, you win

**Option C — Waived Fee for Commitment (MM-24):**
> "Mes a mes tiene un fee de activación de 200K (cubre configuración personalizada del AI). Pero si te comprometes por 6 meses, te lo elimino completo."

This is the RECOMMENDED model for clients 1-7:
- Month-to-month tire-kickers pay 200K upfront (funds your survival)
- Committed clients avoid the fee (longer retention, better LTV)
- Either way, you get paid

### Recommended Approach for Clients 2-7 (Survival Phase):
1. Lead with Option 2 (demo test drive) — FREE, no cost to you
2. If they want to try "for real" → Option A (trial with penalty, 100K fee if conditions broken)
3. Main close → Option C (200K setup month-to-month OR waived with 6-month commitment)
4. If "too expensive" → Option B (pay less now frames 79K as discount)

**Setup fee stays alive. Free trial exists. Both serve you.**

## 5. "LO NECESITO AHORA" BUTTON — Full Psychological Breakdown

### Why it's a GOOD idea:

| Principle | How it applies |
|:----------|:--------------|
| **PS-07 (Consistency)** | Clicking "LO NECESITO AHORA" is a public declaration of need. Once they click, they've self-identified as "someone who needs this NOW." Backing out feels inconsistent. |
| **PS-05 (Scarcity/Urgency)** | The word "AHORA" creates urgency without fabricating it. It says "this is for people who don't wait." |
| **PS-02 (Focal = Causal)** | The intensity of the language draws attention — which means the brain assigns it importance. |
| **MM-31 (Hyper Buying Cycle)** | Some visitors arrive ALREADY sold. They just want to buy. Giving them a fast-track honors the hyper buying moment instead of forcing them through a funnel they don't need. |
| **Self-selection filter** | People who click this CTA are genuinely urgent. They're your HOTTEST leads. Route them straight to WhatsApp or calendar. |

### Why it COULD be a bad idea:

| Concern | Principle | Mitigation |
|:--------|:----------|:-----------|
| Seems desperate/unprofessional | PS-23 (Backlash) | Only if it's the PRIMARY CTA. As a secondary/tertiary, it reads as confidence, not desperation. |
| Attracts impulsive, low-quality buyers | OF-06 (engaged clients) | Mitigated by the setup fee — impulsive clickers still have to pay to start. |
| Doesn't match premium positioning | PS-19 (Fluency) | Depends on design execution. A well-designed urgent CTA on a premium site reads as "exclusive" not "desperate." |

### Verdict: **YES, include it — as a SECONDARY CTA.** 

Layout:
```
[  Ver Demo Gratis  ]        ←  Primary CTA (low barrier, high volume)
[  LO NECESITO AHORA →  ]   ←  Secondary CTA (hot leads, fast track)
```

The primary CTA captures the 80% who need convincing. The secondary captures the 20% who are already sold. You lose nothing by having both — it's just a different entry point to the same funnel.

**Critically:** "LO NECESITO AHORA" must go to a DIFFERENT flow than "Ver Demo Gratis." Hot leads should go straight to WhatsApp chat with you (the human) or a Calendly-style instant booking. Don't make them watch a demo they don't need.

### 4-Week Billing Cycle — Chile Feasibility:

**Technically:** Yes. If you're using:
- **Stripe** (available in Chile since 2024): Custom billing intervals supported natively. Set `interval: 'day'`, `interval_count: 28`.
- **Flow.cl** (Chilean processor): Supports subscription billing with custom intervals. You'd set 28-day periods.
- **Manual transfer** (transferencia bancaria): You just invoice every 28 days instead of the 1st of each month. Most clients won't notice or care.

**Legally:** No Chilean law requires monthly billing. You can bill any interval as long as it's in the contract.

**Practically:** The 13th payment kicks in around month 12. Some clients might notice and ask. Your answer: "El plan siempre ha sido cada 4 semanas — es estándar en la industria SaaS" (it IS standard for many SaaS companies). Put "cada 4 semanas" clearly in the terms of service.

**Revenue impact at 7 clients:** 7 × 79K × 1 extra payment = +553K CLP/year pure profit from changing one word.
